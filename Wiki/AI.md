# 🤖 Artificial Intelligence
### Chat Bots, Text Generators, Image Generators, ChatGPT Tools etc.

## AI Chatbots: 

##### 1. [ChatGPT ⭐ ](https://chat.openai.com)
A very popular AI chatbot that answers all your questions and more.

##### 2. [Phind ⭐ ](https://www.phind.com/)
A very good AI chatbot that can answer all your questions and is a very good programming assistant.

##### 3. [Bard ⭐ ](https://bard.google.com/)
Google's chatbot.

##### 4. [OSSChat](https://osschat.io/)
Has multiple open-source chatbots, good quality chatbots.

##### 5. [Pi.AI ⭐ ](https://pi.ai/talk)
An excellent AI assistant that you can have conversations with, vent with, help you with work etc.

##### 6. [Character.AI ⭐ ](https://beta.character.ai/)
Roleplaying chatbots.

##### 7. [Perplexity ⭐ ](https://www.perplexity.ai/)
An amazing GPT-3.5 chatbot. Great for research.

##### 8. [Poe ⭐](https://www.poe.com)
Multiple chatbots in one app (Claude, ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude-2 etc) all for free, but some paid chatbots like GPT-4 have limited the amount of times you can chat with them for the day.

##### 9. [Replika](https://www.replika.ai)
AN awesome AI that you can talk to and sounds like you are talking to a real person. You can make it your friend, (free) girlfriend, wife, mentor, boyfriend, husband (paid). But most of the features are paid.

##### 10. [Claude ⭐ ](https://www.anthropic.com/product)
Anthropic's AI chatbot.
